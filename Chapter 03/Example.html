Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite food. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ltbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

<01>
  • Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat /li>
  • Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  • Once butter has melted and cream and eggs
  • Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you making an omeletter) /li>
  • When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served) 11
